## Notes on Sources

⚠️ **Source Quality**: This bibliography includes both skeptical/critical sources and pro-parapsychology sources. Readers should note that:

- **Mainstream scientific consensus** rejects remote viewing as lacking credible evidence
- **Pro-parapsychology sources** are included for completeness but are generally not accepted by mainstream science
- **Government evaluations** concluded no operational or intelligence value
- **Critical/skeptical sources** identify significant methodological flaws in pro-parapsychology research

Always evaluate sources critically and consider the scientific consensus.
